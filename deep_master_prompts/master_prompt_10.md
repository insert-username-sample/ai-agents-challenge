# MASTER DISTILLATION PROMPT 010: COMPILER ENGINE & FINAL INTEGRATION

## MACRO-PHASE 1: THE COMPILATION DIRECTIVE
The Compiler Engine is a deterministic micro-service that operates post-MCTS termination. Once Brahma emits the `TERMINAL_STATE` token, the Compiler Engine takes the Drafter's final Markdown AST, the Objector's compliance sheet, and the Verifier's cryptographic evidence ledger, and fuses them into the final deployment payload (PDF/Docx/JSON).
