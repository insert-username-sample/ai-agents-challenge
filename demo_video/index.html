The Challenge of
Generative AI

Modern AI systems are becoming increasingly capable. But they still struggle with two fundamental problems: assumptions and hallucinations. A response can appear correct, even when critical facts were never verified.

Introducing
Clausely AI

Clausely was built to challenge generated outputs before they become conclusions. Built on Google's Agent Development Kit, Clausely combines Gemini 3.5 Flash, Grounding with Google Search, and a coordinated swarm of specialized agents working in real time.

Decentralized Path
Verification

When a user query enters the system, instead of committing to a single answer, Clausely generates multiple possible reasoning paths. Every path is continuously evaluated against grounded evidence, verifying facts throughout the reasoning process.

Swarm Mechanics &
Formal Search

As facts enter, specialized agents begin evaluating paths. Sibling nodes compete and propose options, aligning closely with the verification-driven principles of Google DeepMind's AlphaProof Nexus framework.

Adversarial Play &
Pruning Gates

Rather than trusting the first response, Clausely compares competing possibilities and pressure-tests them. As evidence arrives, unsupported paths are pruned, and grounded paths continue forward.

Human-in-the-Loop
Grounding

When critical information is missing, Clausely does not guess. The system pauses and requests clarification. In one real-world case, a demographic mismatch was caught, eliminating the ungrounded path immediately.

Zero-Hallucination
Compilation

The result is a system designed not only to generate answers, but to verify them. Clausely demonstrates how coordinated AI agents can reduce hallucinations and produce reliable outcomes.
