# Ultra Vision 4: The Simulation Multiverse

## Every Simulation is a Universe
The core of Clausely's adversarial capability is the Long-Horizon Simulator utilizing Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS). In the Ultra Vision paradigm, each time the system generates an MCTS tree of 2000 iterations to test a single case strategy, it is not merely running a loop. It is generating **2000 distinct universes**.

## The AlphaGo/AlphaZero Trajectory
Just as AlphaZero plays out millions of simulated Go universes to discover the ultimate winning move, the 7 canonical strategist agents play out thousands of judicial universes:
*   A universe where the procedural limitation period was challenged.
*   A universe where a single mother's caste standing was dismissed by the registry.
*   A universe where the opponent agent successfully forced an exhaustion of alternative remedies.

## Brahma's Judgment
These universes are inherently unstable. Left unchecked, neural models will hallucinate success across all of them (the "mock simulation 100% accuracy" failure mode). 

This is where the Gods Council steps in. Brahma (The Creator / Mythos Analyzer) oversees the multiverse. If a simulation universe relies on a hallucinated citation or a temporal impossibility (e.g., a retired officer acting as an active investigator), Brahma identifies the flaw and absolutely destroys that universe. It is permanently pruned from the MCTS tree via the $\lambda P_{assumption}$ mathematical penalty.

Only the single, perfectly grounded, procedurally flawless universe survives to become the final strategy recommendation presented to the human lawyer.
