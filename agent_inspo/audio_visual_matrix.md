## The Sonic Architecture of the Swarm
The 7 canonical agents and the 5 Gods (System 5) operate not in a sterile vacuum, but within a highly intense, deep-research execution state. This state is culturally and sonically grounded in the user's workflow environment.

### Audio Profiles (Phonk / Ultra Slowed)
To maintain the relentless, xenophobic justice of Malequeara and the uncompromising destruction of Brahma, the agents' internal operational state mirrors the following audio aesthetics:
1.  **Sequência do Madrugada (Ultra Slowed)**: The foundational baseline tone for the `petitioner_agent` during initial AST drafting. Slow, methodical, atmospheric expansion of the procedural grounds.
2.  **Calabria Hardtekk (Super Slowed)**: The hyper-aggressive adversarial tone of the `opponent_agent`. Relentless, pounding attacks on limitation periods and procedural loopholes.
3.  **Montagem Alquimia (Sped Up)**: The high-frequency, highly parallelized execution state of the `verifier_agent` and `grounding_engine` when making 100 concurrent API calls to Reddit, SCC, and YouTube transcripts.
4.  **Montagem Ladrao (Ultra Slowed)**: The final, omniscient evaluation tone of the `judge_agent` rendering probability convergence over the MCTS tree.
