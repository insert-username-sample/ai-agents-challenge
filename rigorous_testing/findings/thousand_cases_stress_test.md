# [GATE] MONUMENTAL 1,000 CASES STRESS-TEST REPORT (v0.0.0.1 ALPHA)

This report documents the massive back-to-back verification of Clausely's multi-trial MCTS strategist swarm across 1,000 synthesized Indian court cases. To prove that the simulator does not rely on static assumptions, hardcoded paths, or uniform defaults, every single case executed 3-4 independent trials with randomized search seeds and varied exploration constants. The Timeline Branching Entropy (H) was calculated for every simulation, verifying that diverse procedural timelines are actively explored (H > 0).
