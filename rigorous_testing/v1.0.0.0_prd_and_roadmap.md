# PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT & ROADMAP: CLAUSELY V1.0.0.0

---

## 1. Executive Summary & Core Framing

- Current Status: v0.0.0.1 Alpha Toy Prototype
- Target Release: v1.0.0.0 Production-Ready Release
- Key Transition: Deprecate keyword CSE; Deploy semantic RAG grounding search; Onboard pilot legal firms

This document defines the Product Requirements Document (PRD) and complete phased engineering roadmap to transition the Clausely platform from an early **v0.0.0.1 alpha toy prototype** to a commercially viable **v1.0.0.0 production-ready release**.

A crucial learning from the early prototype is that simulated multi-agent runs (such as the legacy 1,200-timeline strategist swarm) function only as conceptual mocks. In practice, the Indian legal system is highly non-linear, procedurally complex, and subject to informal administrative frictions. SOTA generalist models fail at basic factual grounding, often misclassifying the primary petitioner-in-person (Smt. Vidya Khobrekar) as an advocate.

Clausely v1.0.0.0 addresses these gaps by deprecating keyword searches, deploying semantic grounding search verification at every step, scaling tree search depth adaptively based on case complexity, and introducing **Melaquera** (the standalone Conciliator and Mediator) to veto prior-based hallucinations.

## 2. Product Requirements (PRD v1.0.0.0)

### 2.1 Deprecation of Google Custom Search Engine (CSE)
*   **The Mandate**: Google CSE is officially deprecated and is **100% abandoned** in v1.0.0.0. All configuration parameters (`GOOGLE_CSE_KEY`, `GOOGLE_CSE_CX`) are removed.
*   **The Replacement**: Live legal precedent and rule checks must utilize **Gemini Google Search Grounding** (`genai.Client` with `GoogleSearch` tool). This restricts searches semantically to authoritative court databases (`sci.gov.in`, `ecourts.gov.in`, `indiankanoon.org`, `livelaw.in`, `barandbench.com`).
*   **Offline Fallback**: Kept as an unauthenticated DuckDuckGo HTML scraper and local mock database for zero-connectivity situations.

### 2.2 Grounded Strategist Swarm & Dynamic MCTS Depth Scaling
*   **The Mandate**: The strategist swarm is constrained. Before *any* agent within the swarm states a procedural timeline or cites a precedent, a micro-step grounding call must run.
*   **Adaptive AlphaGo-Style Depth Scaling**: Instead of a hardcoded 1,000 runs, the tree-search loop calculates visit iterations dynamically based on case factual complexity, scaling from 57 for simple profiles up to thousands of timelines for complex systems.
*   **Gating Mechanics**:
    *   Runs the 2026 clock validation checks: Smt. Vidya Khobrekar (born 1965) is flagged as **Retired** (from NCSC Investigator) as of 2025 (crossing the age-60 civil service cap).
    *   If a simulation assumes her active status in 2026, it must trigger a `ValueError` and immediately halt downstream execution, preventing carbon and compute waste.

### 2.3 Standalone 8th Agent: Mediator Melaquera
*   **The Mandate**: We introduce a standalone agent, codenamed **Melaquera** (the Conciliator and Mediator).
*   **Role**: Melaquera operates outside of the 7-agent strategist swarm. He acts as a double-layer safety net above the Judge agent, possessing complete veto authority to intercept trajectories and override neural prior assumption failures, ensuring 100% grounded instruction-following.

### 2.4 Strict Command Compliance Checks
*   **The Mandate**: Integrates post-generation validators to ensure draft outputs follow explicit formatting constraints, regional bench registry rules, and correct party standing classifications.

## 3. Engineering Roadmap: Phased Milestones

```
 [v0.0.0.1 Alpha] ──> [v0.0.2 Sandbox] ──> [v0.0.8 Beta] ──> [v1.0.0.0 Production]
     (Current)          (MCTS Gate)        (Pilot Telemetry)    (IndiaLaw Fine-Tune)
```

### Phase 1: MCTS & Grounding Verification (v0.0.2 to v0.0.5)
*   **Milestone**: Implement the modified Upper Confidence Bound for Trees (UCT) equation to penalize statistical neural priors:
    $$UCT(node) = \frac{W_i}{N_i} + c \sqrt{\frac{\ln N_p}{N_i}} - \lambda P_{assumption}$$
*   **Action**: Deploy the adaptive AlphaGo-style dynamic depth visits scaling and integrate Mediator **Melaquera** above the Judge Agent to override prior-based hallucinations.
*   **Acceptance Criteria**: The simulator successfully prunes branches that fail temporal or precedent verification checks, reducing carbon emissions of wasted swarms to exactly zero.

### Phase 2: Pilot Telemetry & Harness Integration (v0.0.6 to v0.0.9)
*   **Milestone**: Deploy the **Clausely Legal Harness** to evaluate role, jurisdiction, and temporal compliance across Gemma 4 E4B, Step-Fun 2.7 Flash, and Kimi K2.6.
*   **Action**: Deploy the telemetry capture engine across the first 50 pilot law firms in India.
*   **Telemetry Logs**:
    *   `+1.0`: Advocate exports the generated draft without edits.
    *   `-1.0`: Advocate deletes or manually overrides an assumed tag (e.g. changing "Advocate" back to "Petitioner-in-person").
*   **Acceptance Criteria**: Collect 10,000 preference pairs to form the Clausely Legal Corpus.

### Phase 3: Fine-Tuning & Production Launch (v1.0.0.0)
*   **Milestone**: Fine-tune Step-Fun 2.7 Flash / Kimi K2.6 using QLoRA and DPO.
*   **Action**: Deploy **IndiaLaw-v1** as the primary legal model, outperforming Claude Sonnet and Harvey in Indian-specific legal drafting.
*   **Acceptance Criteria**: Harness pass rate $\ge 98\%$ on role classifications and $\ge 99\%$ on active criminal statutory (BNS/BNSS/BSA) compliance.

## 4. Pilot Lawyer Onboarding Checklist

To secure high-fidelity preference pairs, Clausely will pilot with 50 specialized civil and administrative firms in Maharashtra.

```
 [Onboard Pilot Firm] ──> [Integrate Telemetry] ──> [Monitor Counter Checks]
                                                            │
                                                            ▼
                                                 [Extract DPO Pref Pairs]
```

1.  **Configure Local SQLite Cache**: Each pilot firm's local Clausely client compiles an encrypted `telemetry_buffer.db` recording every clause edit, role change, and document revision.
2.  **Telemetry Sync Protocol**: On successful document generation or court filing, the client securely uploads the DPO preference pairs (Original vs. Corrected text) to the Clausely developer corpus.
3.  **Feedback Matrix**: Captures court registry objection sheets (rejection reasons, stamp errors) to continuously update the adversarial harness test suites.
